Solutions for Everyday People

If most people commit to doing certain things, we could minimize the effct of global warming. These are things we can all do to reduce the impact of global warming.

Solar Panels

Energy production and burning fossil fuels are responsible for 34% of greenhouse gas emissions. If we relied on solar panels, it would not only be much cheaper but it would also drastically reduce greenhouse gases in our atmosphere.

Public Transport

Cars account for almost 28% of greenhouse gas emissions. Using public transport would significantly bring down that number and reverse the effect of global warming.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

If we reduce the amount of waste and try to reuse items and recycle items, we could stop waste from going to landfills. Landfills are the third largest source of greenhousse gas emissions with almost 13% of emissions being from them.

Solutions for Government

If governments implement certain policies, we could drastically reduce most effects of global warming. These are policies that could reduce the effects of global warming, if implemented.

Re-enter the Paris Agreement

Re-entering the Paris agreement will lower greenhouse gases by 66%, according to numerous studies. This will get us a 1/3 of the way to stopping global warming and, if combined with other solutions, we could bring climate change to the normal fluctuation range of +/- 2Â° C.

Implement Energy Production Laws

Implementing a simple law such as requiring corporations to replace 50% of their non-renewable resources to renewable resources by a specific date would reduce a lot of greenhouse gases. Energy production is responsible for 34% of greenhouse gas emissions and industries are responsible for another 24%. Enforcing laws like these will bring both those numbers down.
